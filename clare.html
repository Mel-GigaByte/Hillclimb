About Clare

Meet Clare Vale, a seasoned racer with a diverse lineup, including a Subaru BRZ and Ford Mustang drift car.

Since 2005, Clare has conquered Wesbank V8s and National drifting, earning the title of the first female podium finisher.

Sponsored by The Truck Man, Wynn's Oil SA, and many more, Clare is not just about racing; she supports causes like The Ann van Dyk Cheetah Centre and road safety.

With a motto of "Stay humble," Clare encourages aspiring female racers to fearlessly pursue their passion.

She's gearing up for Driftmasters SA, Ilamuna Endurance series, and potential off-road races.

Clare's commitment to giving back led her to join the Simola Hillclimb Ladies Team, where she uses racing to make a positive impact.
