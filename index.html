About the Team

The Hillclimb Ladies Team stands as a trailblazing collective of female racers, fusing social responsibility with the thrilling world of high-octane motorsport.

Emerging from the iconic Jaguar Simola Hillclimb in South Africa, this extraordinary team transcends conventional racing norms.

Annually, these devoted female drivers take to the track at the Simola Hillclimb, not just to chase victory but to elevate their mission of raising funds and awareness for their Community in need.

Beyond the exhilarating roar of engines, the Hillclimb Ladies Team is on a mission to break barriers and overcome biases. They actively inspire and propel women into a traditionally male-dominated sector.

Why Support the Hillclimb Ladies Team?

Supporting the HillClimb Ladies Team is not just about backing a group of skilled female racers; it's about championing a cause that extends far beyond the racetrack.

The Significance of Our Mission


Empowerment in Motorsport

Supporting these ladies will break down barriers for future female racers by encouraging gender diversity in a sport that has traditionally been dominated by men.


Community Impact

Your generosity goes beyond racing by donating to the Knysna Community. Helping those in need.


Legacy of Change

Supporting the team helps to ensure that they have a long-term influence on the track and in the community.

GET INVOLVED

The Hillclimb Ladies Team, which consists of tough female drivers, is dedicated to more than just competitiveness and speed. Their goal for the Simola Hillclimb is to inspire and impact significant change in their community as well as on the course. You can participate in their inspirational journey in the following ways:

Donate to the Team or Individual Drivers: Your financial assistance can help fund race and equipment costs, allowing the team to focus on their performance and charitable goals.


Sponsorship Opportunities: Companies and individuals can sponsor the complete team or a single driver. Sponsorships can take many different forms, including financial assistance, services, products, and equipment.


Promotional Support: Help spread the news about the team's activities and cause. Share their story on social media, in your community, and with your contacts to help them acquire awareness and impact.
