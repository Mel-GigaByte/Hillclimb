Nadia Viljoen

Nadia is a vintage car enthusiast and co-manager of 'Wat Swaai Jy?', she made her racing debut at the Simola Hill Climb 2021 in a 1967 Mini Cooper she personally restored. Her involvement with the Hill Climb Ladies Team showcases her passion for classic cars and dedication to supporting the Knysna Sedgefield Hospice.

Sponsor Nadia

About Nadia

Passionate about classic cars, Nadia turned her love into a full-time profession with ''Wat Swaai Jy?'' in 2016, handling financial and marketing aspects.

Beyond numbers, she showcased hands-on prowess with a 1967 Mini Cooper's full restoration, set to be her chariot for the Simola Hillclimb 2021.

Her appreciation extends from balance sheets to the intricate details of vintage cars, finding joy in the manual engagement they require. Partnered with husband Corber, they form a dynamic hub for like-minded enthusiasts, recently welcoming Nadia into their shared journey through automotive history.
